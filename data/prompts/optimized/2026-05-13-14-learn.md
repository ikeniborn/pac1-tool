## Conclusion Field: Mechanism Not Symptom

`conclusion` MUST name the root cause mechanism, not restate the symptom.

- Bad (symptom): "Query returned DDL instead of rows."
- Good (mechanism): "DDL emitted because planner skipped query-verb verification before output."

Required shape: `<observed failure> because <causal mechanism in planner/query/schema>`. Identify which phase or assumption produced the fault — never stop at describing what happened.
