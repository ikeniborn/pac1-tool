## Reasoning Field Schema

`reasoning` MUST contain three explicit parts — reject single-char or placeholder outputs:

1. **Verbatim error**: quote exact error message or empty-result signal from SQL execution (e.g. `"0 rows returned"`, `"no such column: product_id"`).
2. **Cause locus**: name the offending column, value, or join predicate (e.g. `pp.key='diameter' filtered value_text instead of value_number`).
3. **Corrective hypothesis**: concrete fix proposal tied to cause (e.g. `switch to value_number; re-run with numeric comparator`).

Validation: if `reasoning` lacks any of three parts, or is shorter than 40 chars, or contains placeholder tokens (`...`, `TODO`, `?`), output is invalid. Regenerate before emit.
