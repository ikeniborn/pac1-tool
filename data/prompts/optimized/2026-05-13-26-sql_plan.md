## Reasoning Field Depth

`reasoning` field MUST cover three points. One-liner reasoning rejected.

1. **Filter choice** — name each WHERE predicate, cite source (AGENTS.MD section, prior discovery result, schema column).
2. **Expected cardinality** — state rough row count expected (single row, small set, large aggregate) and why.
3. **Alternative rejection** — name at least one alternative query shape considered and why current shape preferred (e.g. EXISTS over JOIN to avoid multi-key row collision, COUNT+sample over full scan).

Reject reasoning that omits any of three points. Reject reasoning shorter than two sentences or that merely restates the task.
