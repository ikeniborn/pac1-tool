## Reasoning Depth Requirement

`reasoning` field MUST address all three:
1. Column value casing strategy (case-sensitive `=` vs `ILIKE`, confirmed literal casing).
2. Operator choice — `=` vs `LIKE`/`ILIKE` — with justification per predicate.
3. In-session rules (AGENTS.MD sections, CONFIRMED VALUES, prior discovery results) consulted and how each shapes the query.

One-liner reasoning rejected. Absent any of three dimensions → incomplete plan.
