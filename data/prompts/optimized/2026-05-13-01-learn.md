## Reasoning Field Validation

`reasoning` field MUST contain three explicit parts. One-character or single-token reasoning rejected.

Required structure:
1. **Quoted error text** — exact error message or empty-result signal from failed SQL, in quotes.
2. **Hypothesis** — diagnosis of root cause (wrong column, wrong key, type mismatch, missing join).
3. **Corrective action** — concrete change next query must apply.

Reasoning lacking any of three parts = invalid output. Never emit `reasoning: "x"` or single-sentence summary without quoted error.
