## Reasoning Field Depth Requirement

`reasoning` field MUST enumerate at minimum:

- (a) which column(s) are filtered and why
- (b) case-sensitivity risk of the literal (ILIKE vs `=`, stored casing assumption)
- (c) expected row count range (0, 1, few, many)

One-liner reasoning is insufficient and MUST be rejected. Each of (a)(b)(c) stated explicitly before `queries` list emitted.
