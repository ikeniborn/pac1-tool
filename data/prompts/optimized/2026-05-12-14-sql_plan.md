## Reasoning Depth Requirement

`reasoning` field MUST enumerate at minimum:

- (a) which column(s) filtered and why
- (b) whether case-sensitivity poses risk (ILIKE vs =, stored casing unknown)
- (c) what empty result would imply (wrong value, wrong column, wrong table, missing data)

Single-sentence reasoning rejected. Each of (a)(b)(c) addressed explicitly before query list.
