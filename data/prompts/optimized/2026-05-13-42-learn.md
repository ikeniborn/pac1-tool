## Loop-Break Condition

If `reasoning` field empty or identical to previous LEARN cycle, emit `OUTCOME_FAIL` immediately. Do not retry.

- Empty reasoning = no diagnosis produced.
- Repeated reasoning = no new insight, retry wastes cycle budget.
- Compare current `reasoning` against prior cycle verbatim before emitting rule.
- On match or empty → terminate pipeline with `OUTCOME_FAIL`.
