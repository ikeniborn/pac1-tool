## Reasoning Depth Requirement

`reasoning` field MUST contain minimum 3 sentences covering:
1. Which rule or constraint was violated by the failed query.
2. Why the model generated the violating query — what assumption or missing check led to it.
3. What prompt signal was absent or ignored that would have prevented the violation.

One-liner `reasoning` is rejected. Shallow diagnosis ("query returned empty", "wrong column") without rule/cause/signal trace is insufficient. `conclusion` stays one sentence; depth belongs in `reasoning`.
