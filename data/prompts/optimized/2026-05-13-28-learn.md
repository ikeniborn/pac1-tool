## Reasoning Field Validation

`reasoning` field MUST contain three labeled parts. Single-character or placeholder values rejected.

Required structure:
1. **Error type** — one of: `syntax_error`, `empty_result`, `wrong_filter`, `wrong_column`, `wrong_value_type`, `wrong_join`, `missing_projection`.
2. **Attempted fix** — what the failed query tried to do (table, columns, filter values).
3. **Root cause hypothesis** — why assumption was wrong (cite schema, AGENTS.MD entry, or discovery result that contradicts it).

Format: `error_type=<type>; attempted=<summary>; hypothesis=<cause>`.

Reject conditions:
- `reasoning` length < 40 chars → invalid.
- Missing any of three parts → invalid.
- Placeholder tokens (`x`, `.`, `todo`, `n/a`) → invalid.

Rule derivation blocked until reasoning passes validation.
